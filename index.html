Vision

To achieve excellence as a hub for exploring, educating, and advocating renewable energy, with the goal of providing the university community with the necessary knowledge to excel in research, education, and community engagement within the transformative realm of renewable energy and tools to play a leading role in shaping sustainable solutions.

Mission
